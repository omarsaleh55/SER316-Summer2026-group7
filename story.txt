Omar walked down the Hauptstrasse and stopped at a Baeckerei to grab some Gebaeck before his big adventure began.He noticed a Biergarten across the street and decided to stop for a cold drink before heading to the Flughafen.
At the gate, a man in Lederhose was eating a Bratwurst and reading a map of Schloss Eltz.
Suddenly, a Hund ran through the gate wearing a tiny Dirndel, grabbed the man's Bratwurst, and disappeared into a shiny Porsche parked outside.  One could hear Rammstein playing from the Porsche, and the side of the Porsche had a wettbewerbsfaehig sign of a Hund.
